## Subhead

will cost, and what to do next. If you need it, we'll guide you through every step and fill in your
forms. You only pay when you're ready to submit.

## Primary CTA

**Check if you need probate** → (starts the free assessment, no signup to see the result)

## How it works (3 steps)

1. **Tell us about the estate.** A few questions about the will, the property, and the accounts. Takes
   about five minutes.
2. **Get your answer and your plan.** We tell you if probate is needed, whether there's inheritance tax
   to deal with, what it'll cost, and the exact steps. If your estate needs a professional, we'll say so.
3. **We do the heavy lifting.** Follow the guided steps and we fill in your probate and inheritance tax
   forms from your answers. Pay one fixed fee only when you're ready to submit.

## Price section

**One fixed fee. No percentage. No surprise bill.**
Using ProbateSwift is free until your forms are ready to submit. Then it's one fixed fee, agreed
upfront. A solicitor doing the same inheritance tax forms typically charges around £1,290, and banks
take 2 to 4 percent of the whole estate. We don't.
