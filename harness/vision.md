## Vision

Probate is a painful and complicated process that more and more families will have
to complete, given recent changes to the legal framework. ProbateSwift (a.k.a. Probate
Navigator) helps people get a **personalised, guided probate service without the price
tag** that solicitors and professional estate-administration firms currently charge.

It sits deliberately in the gap between three poor options people face today: free but
generic government guidance (GOV.UK), expensive solicitor-led services, and shallow
checklist articles. ProbateSwift is the structured, affordable, guided middle: clear
enough for a grieving non-expert to act on, honest about when a case is too complex for
self-service, and cheap enough to be a real alternative to paying a professional.

## Scope (initial)

England & Wales only. Coverage ambition is **broad self-serve** — push as wide as is
safely self-serviceable, including intestacy (no will) and some IHT-payable estates, not
just the narrowest happy path. The hard limits remain genuine complexity: major dispute,
insolvency, complex trusts, foreign-probate complexity, or contested estates, which are
triaged out to specialists.

The product guides users to one of three honest outcomes:
1. Probate is not required.
2. Probate is required → proceed through a guided application journey.
3. The case is too complex/risky for self-service → seek specialist legal support.
