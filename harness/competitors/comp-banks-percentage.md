## Positioning
Banks and many traditional firms charge **2–4% of estate value** — £6,000–£12,000+ on a £300k estate.
Often the "default" when an executor doesn't know better or wants it off their plate.

## Strengths
Convenience; perceived safety; bundled with existing banking relationship.

## Gaps ProbateSwift exploits
Percentage pricing is widely seen as poor value and is the **single clearest target** for a flat-fee
disruptor. Every "£8,000 to administer a simple estate" story is a ProbateSwift acquisition message.
The whole product narrative ("without the price tag") is aimed squarely here.
