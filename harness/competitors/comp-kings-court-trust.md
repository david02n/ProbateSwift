## Positioning
Specialist probate/estate-administration firm, 40,000+ families served, transparent fixed fees incl.
VAT + registry fee, dedicated "Personal Estate Manager", ICAEW-regulated. Done-for-you, premium-trust.

## Strengths
Deep specialism; human estate manager; transparent fixed fees; partnerships (e.g. InheritNOW for
bereaved-family finance).

## Gaps ProbateSwift exploits
Full-service price point and human-led model; not a self-serve product. ProbateSwift's wedge is the
self-serve tier below this — and again, the complex-estate triage-out is a referral fit, not a contest.
