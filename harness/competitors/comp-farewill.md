## Positioning
The leading consumer online-probate brand. Fixed fees, explicitly positioned against percentage
charging: "Essential Probate" from **£595 (+£273 registry fee)**; "Complete Probate" from **£1,500**.
Strong brand, will-writing funnel feeding probate.

## Strengths
Trusted brand; transparent fixed pricing; clear cheaper-than-banks narrative; full-service option.

## Gaps ProbateSwift exploits
"Essential" is still grant-focused, not a guided end-to-end self-serve journey; "Complete" jumps to
£1,500 (done-for-you). Room beneath/between these tiers for a **lower flat fee, genuinely self-serve
but heavily guided** product — especially the IHT-form and pre-submission-validation pain that
Farewill's cheap tier doesn't fully remove. ProbateSwift's direct head-to-head competitor — pricing
and coverage must be benchmarked against it.
