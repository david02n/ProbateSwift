## Positioning
Large, trusted high-street brand offering **fixed-fee** (and historically percentage) probate, fully
**SRA-regulated** — a credibility edge over IPW-membership-only providers. "No upfront cost" framing.

## Strengths
Brand trust; full regulation; reassurance for nervous, grief-stricken users; done-for-you service.

## Gaps ProbateSwift exploits
Solicitor-led / done-for-you cost base; not a low-cost self-serve tool. ProbateSwift competes on
price and autonomy for simpler estates, triaging the genuinely complex ones toward firms like this.
**Regulatory note:** Co-op's SRA status is a trust signal ProbateSwift must answer (positioning as
guidance/tooling, not reserved legal activity) — flagged as a risk.
