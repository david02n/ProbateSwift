## Opportunity
A single structured dashboard for the whole job: who's involved, which assets exist, which documents
are collected, and which institutions still need notifying — reducing the fragmented, repeated-effort
burden that makes death admin so stressful.

## Desired outcome
Lower cognitive/emotional load and fewer dropped balls; a calmer, more "held" experience that builds
the trust needed to convert and to justify a fee.

## Why it's worth it
Addresses the most strongly-evidenced pain (87% stressed / 40% mental-health decline) and extends
ProbateSwift beyond the grant into the broader estate-admin journey where Settld/Empathy are proving
demand. Differentiates on experience, not just price.

## Open assumptions & risks
- Risk of scope creep beyond core probate; sequence after the triage/IHT/validation core.
- Institution-notification integrations (banks/utilities) may need partnerships (cf. Settld).
