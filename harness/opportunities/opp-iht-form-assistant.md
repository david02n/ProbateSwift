## Opportunity
Turn the adviser-grade IHT400 and its schedules into a plain-language, validated, step-by-step flow:
detect which schedules are needed (IHT405/IHT403/etc.), guide valuations, prompt the IHT reference
before submission, and flag the 6-month tax deadline.

## Desired outcome
Laypeople complete correct IHT paperwork without a solicitor — avoiding the up-to-£3,000 penalties,
7.75% interest, and weeks of rework that mis-filed forms cause.

## Why it's worth it
Directly attacks the most-cited "not fit for purpose" pain; the 2027 pensions-into-IHT change expands
the affected population (~10,500 newly-liable estates/yr + threshold drag), so demand is growing, not
shrinking. High differentiation vs GOV.UK and even vs Farewill's cheap tier.

## Open assumptions & risks
- Tax-form guidance has accuracy/liability stakes; must be kept current with HMRC rules (incl. 2027 changes).
- Boundary between "help completing a form" and regulated tax advice needs care.
