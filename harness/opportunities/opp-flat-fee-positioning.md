## Opportunity
Offer a transparent low **flat fee** sitting in the £300→£1,500 gap — well under banks' 2–4%
(£6k–£12k+) and under Farewill's £1,500 "Complete" tier — for genuinely self-serve-but-guided cases.

## Desired outcome
Convert price-sensitive, capable executors who today either risk DIY or overpay a firm. Directly
serves goal 2 (paying customers) and goal 3 (lower cost).

## Why it's worth it
The percentage-fee model is the clearest value target in the market; "without the price tag" is the
core brand promise. A predictable flat fee is itself a differentiator vs opaque % quotes.

## Open assumptions & risks
- **Exact price point is still needs_user_input** — set it after benchmarking Farewill/Co-op and
  testing willingness-to-pay vs the free £300 DIY route.
- Must justify the fee over free GOV.UK by experience (triage + IHT help + validation), not features alone.
