## Problem
A large share of probate applications are "stopped" by HMCTS for missing/incorrect information, and
stopped cases take far longer to clear.

## Pain
Who: self-serve and even professional applicants. How often: a substantial minority of all
applications — ~25% (~8,000) stopped due to IHT issues; ~15,000 cases stopped in a year over missing/
incorrect executor info; others over missing death certificates, unsigned/absent original wills, and
name discrepancies. How badly: stopped applications take "more than double" the admin time; stopped
queue ~21 weeks vs ~5 weeks for a clean digital file; >2,000 applications took over a year (up from
737 in 2020/21). Workaround: trial-and-error, or pay a professional to get it right first time.

## Evidence
HMCTS/ICAEW data: IHT-related stops ~25%/~8,000; executor-info stops ~15,000; named reasons include
missing certified death certificate, original/unsigned will, name mismatches, and applying before
HMCTS receives IHT421 (~21-day lag). Clean digital files clear in ~5 weeks vs ~21 weeks for stops.
