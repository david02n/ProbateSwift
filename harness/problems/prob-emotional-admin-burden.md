## Problem
Probate sits inside a wider, fragmented estate-admin job — notifying banks, utilities, subscriptions,
creditors — done while grieving, with no single tracker of who/what/which documents.

## Pain
Who: the bereaved executor/family. How often: throughout the months of administration. How badly:
**87%** of recently bereaved found death admin stressful; **40%** saw mental health decline (women
61% vs men 38%); **36%** found it harder than expected; law firms report **64%** of clients become
visibly stressed and **39%** needed emotional support. Workaround: spreadsheets, repeated calls,
notification services (e.g. Settld), or paying it all to a firm.

## Evidence
Legal Futures survey (87%/40%/36%/64%/39%); Empathy and Settld both built around this exact burden;
"notify once, notify many" industry pushes. Strong, multi-source evidence of severity.
