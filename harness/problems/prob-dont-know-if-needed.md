## Problem
At the very start, laypeople can't answer the threshold questions: is probate required at all, am I
eligible to apply, which forms/route apply to this estate?

## Pain
Who: nearly every first-time executor/administrator. How often: at the outset of every case. How
badly: causes paralysis, wasted effort down the wrong route, or premature spend on a professional
"just to be sure"; compounds the stop-rate problem when people guess wrong. Workaround: piece it
together from GOV.UK and forums, or pay for reassurance.

## Evidence
PRD documents this as the primary user problem set; bereavement-support and government guidance are
generic and non-personalised. Triage uncertainty is a recognised, repeated theme (also implied by
high stop rates from wrong-route applications). Some specifics (frequency/severity) remain at
researched rather than validated — worth confirming with user interviews.
