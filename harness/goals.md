## Goals

1. **Cover the majority of probate application flows end to end** — support the common
   England & Wales journeys (will exists, eligible applicant, IHT-excepted or clearly
   routable estate) from "do I even need probate?" through to a submitted application,
   including triage-out of cases too complex for self-service.

2. **Generate real, paying customers** — convert guided users into revenue; validate that
   people will pay for structured self-serve probate rather than defaulting to a solicitor
   or abandoning to free GOV.UK guidance.

3. **Reduce the time and cost of probate applications in the UK** — measurably cut both the
   money a family spends (vs. solicitor/professional fees) and the elapsed time/effort to
   complete an application.

> **Confirmed by David, 2026-06-03.** Pricing model = a **fixed, low flat fee** (positioned
> against solicitor/% fees). Exact figure still **needs_user_input** — to be set after
> competitor price benchmarking. "Majority of flows" = **broad self-serve** coverage
> including intestacy and some IHT-payable estates, bounded by genuine complexity.
